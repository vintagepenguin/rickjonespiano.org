about rick.


Rick has taught over 70,000+ private lessons in 26+ years of teaching. At one point, Rick was teaching close to 5,000 piano lessons a year.


He's taught students as young as 4 and as old as 80. It's never too late to learn.


Rick is certified by the National Music Teacher's Association. He is also certified in the famous Suzuki method.


Rick has worked with some of the best pianists in the world, and shares that experience with everyone he teachers.

how it works.


Each week, students get a 30 minute private lesson from Rick. Students are expected to practice 4-6 times per week.


Rick charges by the month. It's $64 per month which works out to be $16 per lesson.


Students are also invited to play at recitals which Rick hosts. Playing at recitals is encouraged. It's a great way to practice performing and let others see the progress you've made.


With his experience, Rick could charge much more, but prefers to keep it affordable so that more people can learn the instrument he loves.
